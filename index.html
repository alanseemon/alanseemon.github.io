About me

Bridging economics, policy, and sustainability

I am an applied economist with a track record in macroeconomic analysis, green transition policy, and sustainable finance. My work spans advisory to multilateral institutions, governments, and major conglomerates — from the UN and World Bank Group to Reliance, Adani, and the UK and Japanese governments.

At EY, I co-authored India's Journey to Net Zero, recognised at the Financial Times Energy Transition Summit 2024. I now lead macroeconomic and policy analysis for India's green economy strategy at the UN Environment Programme, coordinating with NITI Aayog, multiple ministries, and international development partners.

Beyond advisory, I co-founded Economiga — a 4,000+ member pan-India economics community — and organised India's first Monsoon School in Economics. I'm a top 6% scorer in the UGC National Eligibility Test and a frequent speaker at policy forums.
